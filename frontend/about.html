About Credit Path AI

Our Mission

Credit Path AI leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to provide accurate and fair loan approval predictions. Our XGBoost model analyzes multiple financial and personal factors to help lenders make informed decisions.

How It Works

Data Collection

Collect comprehensive applicant information including financial history, employment status, and personal details.

AI Analysis

Our XGBoost model processes the data through multiple decision trees to identify patterns and risk factors.

Prediction

Generate accurate approval/rejection predictions with confidence probabilities for better decision making.

Model Features

  • Credit Score Analysis: Evaluates credit history and score
  • Income Assessment: Analyzes income stability and debt-to-income ratio
  • Employment Verification: Considers employment history and type
  • Loan Purpose Evaluation: Assesses the purpose and risk of the loan
  • Collateral Consideration: Factors in mortgages and co-signers
